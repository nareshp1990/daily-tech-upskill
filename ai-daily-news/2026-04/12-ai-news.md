## 3. Agentic AI & Tooling

### World Models — The Next Capability Frontier
- Consensus from **ICLR 2026**: world models (AI that simulates the environment internally) are 2026's defining technical challenge.
- Unlike LLMs that predict text, world models predict **future states of the world** — enabling long-horizon planning.
- NVIDIA Cosmos, Google DeepMind, and Waymo all shipping production variants.

### NVIDIA Isaac GR00T — Language-Driven Robotics
- **Isaac GR00T** enables robots to interpret natural language instructions and execute **complex multistep physical tasks**.
- Trained with **Cosmos synthetic data** — reduces dependency on expensive real-world robot training data.
- Represents the maturation of **vision-language-action (VLA) models** for physical AI.

### MCP at 97M Installs — Becoming the AI Protocol Standard
- MCP now the default tool-call protocol for Claude Code, Cursor, GitHub Copilot, and Gemini CLI.
- **Agentic AI Foundation (Linux Foundation)** governs the standard — ensuring vendor-neutral evolution.
- Developer adoption accelerating: 97M installs in March 2026 with no signs of plateau.
