## 1. Frontier Model Updates

### Google Gemma 4 — New Open-Weight Family
- Google launched **Gemma 4**, its most capable open model family yet, under **Apache 2.0** license.
- Available in four sizes with **native vision, audio, function calling**, 256K context window, and **140+ language support**.
- Flagship 31B parameter model targets advanced reasoning and agentic workflows.
- One of the most capable fully open-weight families available, targeting both researchers and enterprise.

### Claude Mythos Cybersecurity Milestone
- Anthropic's Claude Mythos model **found a 27-year-old bug in OpenBSD** — a widely deployed open-source OS.
- Anthropic announced a **cybersecurity coalition**, providing **$100M in usage credits** to infrastructure organizations.
- Expanding access through government stakeholders to strengthen defenses against AI-powered cyberattacks.

### Google TurboQuant at ICLR 2026
- Google Research unveiled **TurboQuant**, an algorithm that slashes **KV cache memory overhead** dramatically.
- Two-step process: **PolarQuant** (vector rotation) + **Quantized Johnson-Lindenstrauss** compression.
- Enables models with massive context windows to run far more efficiently — critical for inference cost reduction.
