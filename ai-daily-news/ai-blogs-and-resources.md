## Recommended Daily Routine (15–20 min)

```
Morning (5 min):
  → Scan TLDR AI or The Neuron for headlines

Midday (5 min):
  → Check Hugging Face Papers or r/LocalLLaMA top posts

Evening (10 min):
  → Read one article from Import AI, Ahead of AI, or a lab blog

Weekly (30 min):
  → Listen to one episode of Latent Space or Lex Fridman
  → Browse Papers With Code trending

Monthly:
  → Review The Gradient or Ahead of AI deep-dive
  → Update your "What to Watch" list
```
